Lasagna


Description

A delicious Italian classic loaded with meat and cheese, served up family style

Ingredients

  • Gournd Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesean Cheese
  • Basil
  • Oregano

Instructions

  1. Brown ground beef and set aside to drain
  2. Cook Lasagna noodles until soft
  3. line a baking dish with a single layer of noodles
  4. Top noodles with a layer of meat
  5. Top meat with a layer of cheese
  6. Add another layer of noodles
  7. repeat process for two more layers
  8. top off with rest of cheese
  9. Bake in 350 degree oven for 45 minutes
